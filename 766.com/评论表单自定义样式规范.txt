评论表单自定义样式JS调用由
<script type="text/javascript">
	news.comment('comment'); 
	Comment.loadForm('default','自定义css地址');
</script>

改成：
<script type="text/javascript">
	news.comment('comment','自定义css地址'); 
</script>


只用默认样式（统一新闻页白色样式）也可以只写
<script type="text/javascript">
	news.comment('comment'); 
</script>
