SOUNDOBJECT.playbackRate

SOUNDOBJECT.playbackRate
[number] Determines the speed of the playback rate. 1.0 is default speed. 2 is twice as fast, .5 is half speed, etc. Negative numbers cause the sound to play backwards. 
// The sound will be set to go 50% faster
aud.bgmusic.playbackRate = 1.5;

var currentPlaybackRate = aud.bgmusic.playbackRate;
