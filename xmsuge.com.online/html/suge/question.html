Thank you very much for dropping by!

JinkyArt is a family owned business, and a labour of love both creatively and heartfelt. It began in 2003 as a way to share our memories with family & friends.

Photography has given our family so much joy over the years and if there's anything you should know about me, it’s that I am so thankful for this experience. Working with children has always been my passion and I know in my heart of hearts that if I wasn't a child photographer I would still be working with kids and their families in some way.  Bringing photography and children together has been such an unexpected joy.  Years ago, I studied child care at TAFE and later worked as a child care professional.  I really believe my background has been such a fantastic source of inspiration and understanding.

My heart for photography sits in capturing childhood imagination and the beautiful way that a child's laughter can brighten anyone's day.

I won’t prattle on, because more than any stories, reasons, acclaims or accolades, I would really love for my work to speak for itself.

Thanks again for visiting.

xoxoxo

Barb Uil.
